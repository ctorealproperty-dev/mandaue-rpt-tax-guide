What are the payment deadlines?

You can choose between full annual payment or quarterly installments:

Annual (Full) On or before March 31, 2026 Eligible for 10% discount*
Quarterly Dates Q1: Mar 31 | Q2: June 30
Q3: Sept 30 | Q4: Dec 31
How do I pay via GCash? (Step-by-Step)
  1. Open the GCash App and tap Bills.
  2. Select Government as the category.
  3. Search for Mandaue City Government.
  4. Enter your Tax Declaration Number (TDN) and the amount.
  5. Once paid, save the e-receipt and must email it to cto@mandauecity.gov.ph with your name and property details to update the official records.
Paying through Landbank LinkBiz Portal

Best for owners living outside Mandaue City or those using other bank accounts:

  • • Go to Landbank LinkBizPortal.
  • • Select Mandaue City Government as the Merchant.
  • • Select Real Property Tax as the payment type.
Pro Tip: Ensure you have your Tax Declaration Number ready before starting the process.
What are the requirements for walk-ins?

Visit the City Treasurer's Office at Mandaue City Hall. You will need:

  • Copy of your Latest Tax Declaration.
  • Previous year's Official Receipt (OR).
  • Valid ID (for owner or authorized representative).
